How to play

Help the explorer travel from the green IN flag to the purple OUT flag.

  • Move your mouse through a neighboring space—no clicking needed.
  • On a phone or tablet, drag your finger through the maze.
  • Walls will give you a friendly little bump.
  • Your colorful route stays visible to help you remember.
  • Reach OUT and a brand-new random maze will appear!
